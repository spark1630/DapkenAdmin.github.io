Dapken provides revolutionary medication management through automated clinical pathways.

Our MIT team of pharmacists and engineers have over 40 years of collective experience in the industry. We are passionate about building a company that empowers healthcare providers and their patients.


Patients

Medications are confusing and overwhelming, especially when you start a new medication. Dapken is here to be with you every step of the way. We help you take and understand your medications through personalized messages that can also help you manage side effects and any questions that may come up.

We provide personalized longitudinal patient education, efficacy and side effect monitoring, clinical interventions, and more.

Providers

Our system augments your clinical team and addresses common issues patients run into with new medications. Based on a triage protocol we establish together, it can alert your clinician to intervene.

Understanding patients’ reality with medications on a daily basis helps clinicians adjust the treatments at the earliest opportunity and leads to improved patient experience and health outcomes.
